## Selection principles

- **Vary your layouts.** Don't reuse slide 7 twelve times. A 20-slide deck should have at least 6–8 distinct layouts.
- **Match content density to layout.** A slide with four paragraphs of body text wants layout 16 or 17, not layout 7.
- **One chart per slide maximum.** If you need two, split across two slides.
- **Preserve signature brand elements.** The corner-mark triangles (top right), green underline beneath titles, and "SECTION TITLE" eyebrow are brand signatures. Never remove them from content slides.
